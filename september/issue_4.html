Chevrolet Passenger Series Overhaul

General information

Chevrolet is one of the largest American brands, pretty wide-spread in Sweden. Thus data quality enhancements on Chevy/GMC (as well as on other American brands) are quite crucial, esp. taking into account steady increase of interest from American auditory. Plus, we've got a very proactive American user, who's constantly providing us with pictures of cars which are very rare on this side across the pond. And he's quite meticulous, I must say :)

What has been done?

All the passenger Chevrolet series up till 1956 model year have been completely overhauled having been applied with the latest guidelines regarding both facelifts and powertrain handling. When I'm done, Chevrolet will look like this brand.
