General Motors brands overhaul (Chapter 1 - Chevrolet)

General information

Chevrolet is one of the biggest American brands that features hell a lot of regional subsidiaries with completely different lineups across those. Plus, tideous license agreements with numerous third-party auto makers make handling Chevrolet pretty sophisticated.

What has been done?

The whole GM-DAT lineup (i.e. the whole range of European Chevrolet series) has been processed, including its Asian/LADM counterparts. American series from 1990s have just been processed. We lacked some major series and lots of facelifts there (for example, the whole Lumina APV and Corvette C4 Facelift). It helped to decrease number of unconnected Chevrolet idents by 29%. Just as usual, all the changes imposed include upgrading naming conventions to the latest standards and enhancing engines info with proper engine name and engine code where it's possible.
