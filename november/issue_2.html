VAG rework

General information

We continue our work with VAG, for now only with Volkswagen. It is quite long going process considering the amount of information and various variables.

What has been done?

During November Volkswagen Golf MK4 and Golf MK5 were rewritten. As usual, wgathered all the possible model codes to create the exact patterns for the connections. Moreover, around 15-20 engines per generation were added, all with engine codes according to ERWIN VAG brochures. The work with the MK4 is completed. AS for the MK5, there is a need to upgrade a little the existing rules to achieve more accurate connections.

Current status

Working on final edition for the MK5 rules. MOreover, the model codes for MK6 were already gathered. So, we are ready to continue with the later generation.

Plans for the next month

Aa a minimum plan, we are going to finish MK6 during December. However, we have the abitions to finish all the Golf gens by 2019 ;)
